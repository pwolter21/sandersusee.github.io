Home/PNW

The Pacific Northwest, while not as exotic or flashy as some of the other places I've visited, will always be home. While the rain can be dreary, you can't expect the scenery to water itself. The mountians, forests, lakes, rivers, valleys, huge cities, quaint towns, and all the people that you'll find there leave you looking for your next adventure.

California

This place speaks for itself. Everybody knows a little something about Cali. While the weather can't be beat, the land of the famous has a lot more to share then it appears. I've spent summers in the state in the family RV for as long as I can remeber, doing everything from Santa Monica Pier to catching a game at AT&T Park. But I've also spent enough time there to know you've never seen it all, so there's always something more to do.
